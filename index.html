üéØ What Are Solana Meme Coins?

Solana meme coins are cryptocurrency tokens built on the high-performance Solana blockchain that derive their value primarily from community engagement, social media presence, and cultural relevance rather than traditional utility. Unlike Bitcoin or Ethereum, these tokens often start as jokes or internet memes but can develop significant market value through viral marketing and community adoption.

The Solana blockchain provides the perfect environment for meme coins due to its lightning-fast transaction speeds (up to 65,000 TPS) and extremely low fees (often less than $0.01 per transaction). This makes it ideal for the high-frequency trading and community interactions that meme coins thrive on.
